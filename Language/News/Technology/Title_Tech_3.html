The nightmare that has haunted Apple Mouse users for 9 years

Even though the new iMac has been announced and comes with a new Magic Keyboard with a fingerprint scanner, it still comes with the old Magic Mouse 2, which Apple has been putting the charging port in the worst possible place for years.

The Magic Mouse 2 is 9 years old, and Apple announced it in 2015, but what Apple did that seemed odd was to put the charging port on the bottom of the device. It could be an eyesore when users are busy using it, and the charging cable is already out of reach. To this day, Apple has yet to change the design or location of the Magic Mouse's charging port.
