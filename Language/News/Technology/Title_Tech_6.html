Don't forget to come and see the drone racing competition, a new creation by Cambodian youth this weekend

The drone racing competition at the Aircraft and Technology Exhibition is here, which will take place on November 24, 2024 at the football field of Boeung Trabek High School, Phnom Penh, from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

All spectators and the public can freely watch, with the participation of candidates with skills and potential in technology, especially drone racing. Among them, there are approximately 10 teams of candidates from various high schools and universities in Phnom Penh who will compete fiercely to find the strongest to win the prize in the upcoming drone racing competition.

It is worth noting that recently, after passing the first qualifying round, the ATC Lab team provided additional training and orientation to the candidates who were shortlisted for the competition in all four disciplines, passing strict monitoring and evaluation by the Air & Tech Competition committee to advance to the award-winning competition at the Air & Tech Exhibition at the end of 2024.

Please note that the 2024 Air & Tech Competition has four major disciplines: robotics competition, combat robot competition, piloted aircraft competition, and drone racing competition. The winners of all four disciplines will receive medals, trophies, certificates, and a total prize money of 21 million riel.
