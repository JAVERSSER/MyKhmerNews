OPPO CEO Launches Find N3 Series, Says It Would Be Great If Apple Releases iPhone with Foldable Screen

On October 19, 2023, OPPO announced the launch of its latest foldable smartphone, the OPPO Find N3 Series, in Singapore.
Speaking to reporters from Shanghai Yicai, OPPO's product director Pete Liu said that if Apple starts producing foldable phones, it will be good news for the high-end foldable phone market.
