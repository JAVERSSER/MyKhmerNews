How big is this! The Red Magic 10 Series will pack a 7,000mAh battery as well as...

Finally, the Red Magic 10 Series will officially launch its flagship smartphone on November 13. And what's even more special about the Red Magic 10 series is that it will be equipped with a chip that is also incredibly large.

This flagship smartphone will be powered by Qualcomm's latest chip, the Snapdragon X Elite. On the other hand, the battery size is also huge, reaching 7,000mAh. This battery size has not been used in any smartphone yet, and it can support fast charging up to 100W. As for its appearance, both models will be 7 inches in size with a resolution of 2688 x 1216 pixels along with a refresh rate of 144Hz. On the other hand, if we look at its image, we see that it will use a camera embedded under the front screen, called Under Display.
