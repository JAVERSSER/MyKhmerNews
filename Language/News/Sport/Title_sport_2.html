Rodri reveals the special qualities of Ronaldo and Messi that everyone can overlook

Rodri is the most promising midfielder of the moment and the winner of the 2024 Ballon D'Or, as well as the best midfielder in Manchester City. The midfielder, who is considered the best in the world, has currently suffered an ACL injury and is currently recovering from it, and is expected to be out of action until the end of this season and into the new season.

However, despite not being able to be on the pitch yet, he is still an important person who is constantly questioned. In fact, recently the media asked him what the special features of Ronaldo and Messi are from his point of view, and this view is speaking at a time when both of them are equally good.

Regarding this question, Rodri replied: "For me, Ronaldo is a killer in the box. We don't want him to have the ball when he is in the box because he is a great killer. Messi is the most dangerous player when he has the ball. In short, no matter where he is, as long as he has the ball, he is a danger to his teammates at all times."
