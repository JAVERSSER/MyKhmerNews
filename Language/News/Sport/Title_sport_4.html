Secretary General of the Swimming Federation: Coach training course is a special opportunity for trainees to expand their knowledge

The Cambodian Swimming Federation, on the afternoon of December 9, 2024, at the federation's own location, opened a Level 1 swimming coach training course under the support of Olympic Solidarity (Olympic Solidarity Coaching Course Of Swimming In Level 1).

Mr. Hem Kiri, Secretary General of the Cambodian Swimming Federation, said that in other countries, if you want to participate in various training courses, you have to pay money to learn and expand your knowledge of your skills. But in Cambodia, there is attention from the NOCC, the Ministry of Education, and international federations. They always initiate continuous training courses without considering any budget, meaning that participants only spend their time coming to study.

The swimming expert added that the participants had a special opportunity to work with experienced foreign teachers who were full of international experience, so they had to pay attention and work hard to learn both theory and practice over the course of a few days.
Mr. Hal Kiri hopes that the participants will use the knowledge they learned in this training course to train and expand the pool of male and female swimmers in Cambodia to respond to the federation's past efforts.

He also stated that The Olympic Solidarity Coaching Course of Swimming in Level 1, supported by Olympic Solidarity, was held from December 9 to 13, 2024 at the Federation's location in the National Stadium. A total of 25 participants participated, with an international instructor named Lopez Alvarado Miguel Angel, a Spanish national.
