Please note that Messi is currently playing for Inter Miami in the US Premier League. He is currently 37 years old and was a member of Barcelona between 2004 and 2021.

If we look at Messi's history as a member of the Barcelona team, in September 2000, Messi, who was only 13 years old at the time, went on trial for Barcelona. This trial was for Barca to check whether they should transfer Messi or not. Three months later, the club decided to sign a contract with Messi on December 14, 2000.

Messi has been with Barca since then, making his senior debut in 2004. The player has become a club legend and played for the club until 2021. During that time, Messi played a total of 778 games for the club and scored 672 goals and helped the club win 35 major trophies, including 10 La Liga titles and four Champions League titles. After leaving Barca, the player played for PSG until 2023 and has joined Inter Miami in the United States until now. Messi's departure from Barcelona was a departure that fans around the world could hardly believe would happen which was the most surprising thing for Barca fans and Messi himself because the player had helped the club a lot and there were high expectations that he would stay for the rest of his football career.
