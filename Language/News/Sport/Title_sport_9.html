National and international OFF ROAD vehicles will rock Preah Sihanouk Province from November 28 to December 1, 2024.

The OFF ROAD car racing event, which will take place from November 28 to December 1, 2024, will rock Preah Sihanouk Province, with approximately 60 national and international registrations. The Vice President of the Automobile Sports Federation, His Excellency Tea Siam, said in a press conference this morning that For the national registration, so far, there have been approximately 40 vehicles, while the international registration has approximately 20 vehicles. Of these, the international countries include Thailand, Laos, Vietnam, and China. However, registration is also close to completion, so those who want to participate, please hurry up. The representative of the Motor Sports Federation added that the purpose of organizing the international OFF ROAD car racing event in Preah Sihanouk Province is to expand the promotion of this sport and gain more support, and to help promote the province as a tourist area with beautiful beaches and landscapes to the international community. It is also noted that on the morning of November 18, 2024, at NOCC, the Cambodia Motor Sports Federation and the Off-Road Car Family held a press conference to announce the international Off-Road Car Racing event in Preah Sihanouk Province. The event was also attended by His Excellency Tea Siam, Vice President of the Cambodia Motor Sports Federation and Technical Management Department of the Off-Road Car Family.
