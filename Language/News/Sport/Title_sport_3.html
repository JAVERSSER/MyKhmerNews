Table Tennis Federation: The instructor from Australia has good technique and shared his personal experience with the 25 trainees.

A representative of the Table Tennis Federation said that the Level 1 Coach Training Course, which was supported by the Olympic Solidarity Project, was really good and important because it brought in professional instructors from Australia who have good techniques to train the 25 participants.

Mr. Heng Sokcheat, Acting Deputy Secretary General of the Table Tennis Federation, said that the expert instructor from Australia who came to teach this course has good techniques and shared his knowledge and experience with the 25 participants who came to participate. From what he saw, the expert instructor focused on many techniques such as curling serves, backhands, and various other movements. At the same time, after studying theory, the trainees directly practiced training the youth in the kindergarten.

The federation representative added that the trainees gained knowledge and showed a lot of progress after participating in the Olympic training course. Technically, progress on the international stage is that the instructors taught with care for all trainees. It is also noted that the Olympic Sports Solidarity Course on Table Tennis Coaching Level 1 was held from December 2 to 9, 2024. A total of 25 participants participated and the course officially ended on the afternoon of December 9. The training course was also attended by Mr. Trevor Hirth, an Australian trainer.
