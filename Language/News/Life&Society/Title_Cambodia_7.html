Apple quietly releases new iPad Mini with A17 Pro chip and Apple Intelligence

In a quiet but surprising announcement, Apple has unveiled the latest generation of the iPad mini , now equipped with the powerful A17 Pro chip and new Apple Intelligence technology.

The new model retains its signature compact, portable design with significant improvements in performance, graphics, and AI capabilities that make it even more useful than before.

iPad mini features the A17 Pro chip, which delivers a 30% performance boost over the previous A15 Bionic model.

With a six-core CPU and five GPUs, iPad is capable of handling demanding graphics applications and features four times faster ray tracing technology, making iPad mini ideal for gaming and some apps.

Apple Intelligence, powered by the A17 Pro chip, is a major advancement in AI for iPad mini.

It boosts productivity by providing features like rewriting and summarizing text in apps like Notes and Mail.

Siri is now smarter, better understanding natural language and preserving the context of conversations. The Photos app also benefits from AI-powered features like creating custom videos and removing objects from photos.
