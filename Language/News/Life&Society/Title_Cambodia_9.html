It's clear, much cheaper than before! This is the official price of the company selling the iPhone 16 Pro with a full 1-year warranty.

Falcon Tech KH, the exclusive distributor of Apple in Cambodia, has officially announced the launch of the new iPhone 16 Pro in the Cambodian market. Through a promotional image posted, the company confirmed that the iPhone 16 Pro will be available from September 27, 2024 onwards.

The starting price for the iPhone 16 Pro is $1,289 USD, and for purchases from an official distributor in Cambodia, the phone will come with a full-year warranty.

The company is also offering short-term installment plans, with payments as low as $97 per month.

Interested customers can wait for further announcements from Falcon Tech KH regarding pre-orders and other details.
