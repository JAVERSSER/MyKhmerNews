For this feature, it is called Disappearing Messages, and Facebook has released it for a long time to use With its role:

  • Deletes messages we correspond with in Messenger within 24 hours without leaving a trace
  • Has the role of notifying when someone clicks on our Screenshot in Messenger
  • Not only does it take a screenshot, but it also records a video, it also tells us

    • + How to enable

  • Go to someone's Chat in Messenger Then click on their Profile
    Swipe up a little and click on the Disappearing Messages feature and enable this feature After enabling, it will activate this feature immediately

Please note that this feature only works on Chats that we have enabled.
Those who have not enabled this feature can still use the normal features.
